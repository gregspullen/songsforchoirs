How should I print my sheet music?

Our engraving takes page turns into account. Before printing check the page numbers at the top of your score.

Many of our scores begin on page 2, which is designed to appear on the left-hand page when printed double-sided.

For the clearest image, we recommend selecting “Fit to page” (or your printer’s equivalent) before printing.

Do I get a discount for larger orders?

Yes. The price per copy reduces automatically as you increase the number of licensed copies.

Can I download the audio?

Yes — if your browser allows it you are welcome to save the audio for reference. Right click the audio player and 'Save Audio as'.

The recordings are there to help you get to know the music and support rehearsal. If you enjoy the piece, please consider
supporting the project by purchasing licensed copies of the score for your choir.

How do I print a front cover (title page)?

Right click on any title page in preview and 'Save Image as'

Go to print and select 'fit to page'(or your printer’s equivalent). That's it
