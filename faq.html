How can I support the creation of new choir music?

Become a Friend or Patron. SongsforChoirs exists to create original choral music that is accessible and affordable whilst supporting  
the ongoing output of working composers. Friends receive our monthly newsletter while Patrons also receive a 50% discount on all
sheet music for just £1 per month. Every membership helps support the creation of original music, publications and future projects.

Do I get a discount for larger orders?

Yes — The price per licensed copy reduces automatically as the number of copies increases.
Sets of works work out considerably cheaper than buying each item individually.
Patrons also receive a 50% discount on all sheet music purchases.

How should I print my sheet music?

Before printing check the page numbers at the top of your score. Many of our scores begin on page 2 so that they appear
correctly when printed double-sided. For best results select "Fit to page" (or your printer's equivalent) before printing.

How do I print a front cover (title page)?

Right click on any title page in preview and 'Save Image as'. Go to print and select 'Fit to page' (or your printer’s equivalent). That's it.

Can I download the audio?

Yes — if your browser allows it you are welcome to save the audio for reference. Right click the audio player and choose 'Save Audio as'.
