We believe that great choral music should be within reach of every choir.

Our aim is simple: to offer beautifully crafted, enjoyable music at a price that feels fair — and sustainable.

We take care of the writing and engraving; you produce the copies in the way that works best for your choir.

Each purchase provides a licensed set of copies, watermarked with your order number. This watermark is our only protection — and our trust. It allows us to keep prices low while continuing to create and share new music.

If you need more copies, simply place another order. There are no bulk discounts — just a consistent, affordable price for everyone.

We know that rehearsals are busy and practicalities aren’t always perfect. If you have any issues with downloading or printing, just get in touch — we’re here to help.

Songs for Choirs is built on a simple idea: trust, accessibility, and a shared love of singing. If something doesn’t work, we want to know — so we can keep improving it together.

— The Songs for Choirs Team
