We are musicians so we understand how choirs often struggle to buy new music. It’s a major budget concern — so we aim to bring you approachable, enjoyable works at the most competitive price we can offer.

We are supplying the composition: you provide the paper, ink, and everything else that goes into producing your vocal scores. Your purchases allow us to continue creating original works at the lowest possible cost.

Our music downloads in bundles of between 3 and 24 copies so that you can order as close as possible to the number of copies you truly need. There are no price breaks for larger orders — this is simply the most practical way to distribute our music securely and affordably.

Every download is watermarked with a unique ID. This watermark is our only security — and also our trust. It’s how we keep our prices low so we ask that you respect our copyright. A few “extra copies” here and there can add up quickly when we’re sharing music around the world, so if you need an extra copy or two, please buy them — it’s less than the price of a coffee!

Finally, we understand that printers misfeed and ink runs out — things happen. If you have printing issues, just email us and we’ll help you resolve them. We want happy, singing customers! 🎶
